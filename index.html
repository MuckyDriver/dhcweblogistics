Website Details

Dynamic or Static?
All the websites I make will only be in static form, anything like information pages, posters, tutorials, advertisments are fine for me to make as long as it does not includes any dynamic part of the website. In the near future I hope to be able to make dynamic pages using SQL, PHP and more!

Payments
After your quote is successful and you accept it, I will then start making the website from what designs you wanted the website to have. I will be calculating the price from how much hours of work I have put into the websites. Payment will be only accepted via PayPal!

How long does it take?
This all depends on how much you want to me created; the amount of pages and the amount of designs you want to add on each page. A 16 page website will take from 1-2 weeks depending on my schedule from other projects going at the same time. If the website you quoted does not get finished by 2 weeks then I will respond to you either with the current build or ask you for a extra week to get it done.
